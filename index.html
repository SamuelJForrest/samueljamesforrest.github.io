About me

Hello, my name is Samuel James Forrest.

I am a self-taught front-end developer from the heart of the South Wales valleys, with a passion for learning new technologies and problem solving.

I have always been fascinated by the design process and the intricacies of web development, particularly how an expertly designed site or app becomes more than the sum of its parts, and takes on a life of its own.

I believe that accessibility is the core of good design. I strive in my work to minimize all barrieres to access, while still delivering a product with its own distinct personality.

Success, to me, would be to help people solve real-world problems and, if possible, enjoying a laugh along the way.
